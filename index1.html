How to play

Step 1 - Choose the difficulty and controls.

Step 2 - Press the START button. Simon will give the first signal. Repeat the signal by clicking on the corresponding color or pressing the correspondent key.

Step 3 - Simon will duplicate the first signal and add one. Repeat these two signals like in the previous step.

Step 4 - Simon will duplicate these first two signals and add one.

Step 5 - Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly. After each signal in a sequence, Simon automatically speeds up.

Step 6 - If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, or if you take too long to repeat a signal, Simon responds with a sound. This means you've lost, and the sequence of signals ends.
